Blake's 7

This claims to be the largest B7 site on the web and is certainly very comprehensive. Run by Judith Proctor, who is also the driving force behind Redemption

Gallifrey Base

The biggest Doctor Who fan forum and successor to Outpost Gallifrey. You must register to access it.

Lurkers' Guide to Babylon 5

This site has been around almost since the series first aired. It includes full episode guides and is still updated with airdates, convention information etc.
